Aegis Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Aegis ("we", "our", or "us") is a student productivity application that helps students manage tasks, track assignments, and sync with Google Calendar and Google Classroom.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your Google account email, name, and profile picture when you sign in
  • Calendar Data: Events from your Google Calendar (read-only access)
  • Classroom Data: Course information and assignments from Google Classroom (read-only access)
  • Task Data: Tasks, notes, and due dates you create within Aegis
  • Progress Data: Your XP, level, streak, and achievement data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display your calendar events and classroom assignments in the app
  • Track your productivity progress and streaks
  • Sync your tasks across devices
  • Provide gamification features (XP, levels, achievements)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing Aegis services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Revoke Google Calendar and Classroom access at any time
  • Request data export

7. Google API Services

Aegis's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
